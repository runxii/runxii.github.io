## Project Overview

Habichew is a mobile application that integrates behavioral science, gamification, and AI to help users build sustainable habits through automated task decomposition and an immersive reward system.

### The Challenge

Young adults (ages 18-25) often abandon long-term goals due to "fear of failure" or "vague objectives." While behavioral research offers theoretical solutions, there is a gap in the market for automated, highly engaging tools that translate these theories into practice.

### The Solution

- **AI-Driven Decomposition:** Leverages AI to automatically break down broad habits into manageable daily/weekly micro-tasks, significantly reducing the barrier to entry.
- **Psychological Incentives:** Features a virtual pet system and story progression to provide instant gratification, transforming abstract goals into engaging gameplay.

### My Role

- **Backend Engineer (Node.js):** Architected the core backend workflow. Integrated the **OpenAI API** to power the automated habit decomposition logic, and developed systems for user authentication, task generation, and the game reward engine.

  **DevOps & Deployment:** Utilized Docker for containerization and managed the deployment of the database and backend services on Linux servers to ensure stable performance.

  **UX/UI & Visual Design:** Mapped out application logic and user flows utilizing **FigJam**, and designed all game characters and visual assets using Procreate and Figma.

### Results & Impact

- **Cross-Platform Success:** Successfully deployed and operational on both iOS and Android platforms.

  **User Validation:** Acquired and engaged 100+ beta testers during the graduation exhibition, validating the product concept and technical execution.
